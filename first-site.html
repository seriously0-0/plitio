Meet Plitio,the Canadian online pharmacy that comes to you.

GET STARTED

It’s time for a more convenient, more affordable way to manage your health and prescriptions.

With an online pharmacy, you just sign in and tap the app, and we’ll do the rest.
Take your pharmacy needs online to skip the line and save money. Our knowledgeable pharmacists take care of every detail and refill, so you can focus on the things that matter most.

Step 1: Contact Your Pharmacy

How does it work? We simply contact your pharmacy to have prescriptions transferred over to Plitio.

Step 2: Connect with your insurance provider

For added convenience, we deal directly with your insurance provider to process payments.

Step 3: Order your first prescription

Since we’re online, we can pass our overhead savings onto you. And we deliver right to your doorstep, so you save time, too.
