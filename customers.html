With an online pharmacy, you have so much less to worry about.

Plitio was established by two Canadian pharmacists who wanted to make getting prescriptions filled less time-consuming. In turn, this makes customers’ lives less hectic, reducing stress while simultaneously lessening costs.

The trusted staff at Plitio:

Prepare all forms of medication (including pills, capsules, creams, drops, insulin and inhalers)

Delivers for free, when you want, right to your door

Sets up automatic refills and can contact your doctor to request more

Works with your insurance provider, so payments are automatic

Ditch waiting in line by going online.

With Plitio, you’ll shorten your to-do list because our online pharmacy is always open.No more dashes to the drug store or waiting in line.

Manage your prescriptions using the app, pay less for prescriptions, and set up free delivery at a time that works for you.

With Plitio, you’ll have round-the-clock access to your prescription information and access to a dedicated team of pharmacists and assistants.
